# Executive Business Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is a Power BI Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, sales team effectiveness, and operational efficiency.

The dashboard transforms raw sales and customer data into actionable insights through KPI reporting, interactive visualizations, drill-through analysis, and business storytelling.

---

## Business Objectives

The project was designed to answer key business questions:

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which sectors contribute the most to business growth?
* Which sales regions and managers drive performance?
* Who are the most valuable customers?
* Where do operational bottlenecks exist in the sales pipeline?
* How can decision-makers improve overall business performance?

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of:

* Total Revenue
* Win Rate
* Deal Cycle Analysis
* Product Performance
* Revenue Trends
* Sector Contribution

### 2. Sales Team Performance

Analyzes:

* Regional Office Performance
* Manager Contribution
* Sales Agent Performance
* Revenue Distribution

### 3. Customer & Industry Analysis

Explores:

* Customer Revenue Distribution
* Industry Performance
* Top Customer Accounts
* Customer Size vs Revenue Relationships

### 4. Operational Efficiency

Monitors:

* Deal Pipeline Health
* Deal Cycle Duration
* Win vs Lost Deal Analysis
* Operational Insights

### 5. Deal Detail Explorer

Provides:

* Interactive Drill-Through Analysis
* Account-Level Investigation
* Deal-Level Performance Monitoring

---

## Key Insights

* Retail emerged as the highest revenue-generating sector.
* GTX Pro was identified as the top-performing product.
* West Region contributed the largest share of company revenue.
* Technology and Retail sectors dominated customer revenue.
* Engaging opportunities remained active significantly longer than closed deals, indicating potential operational bottlenecks.
